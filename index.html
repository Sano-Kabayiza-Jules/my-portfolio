Biography

Sano Kabayiza Jules is an accomplished entrepreneur, IT professional, and student based in Rwanda. As the co-founder of ViewHome, a revolutionary real estate platform that offers zero-commission services for buying, selling, and renting properties, Sano has been instrumental in transforming the real estate landscape in Rwanda and aims to expand its reach across Africa. He is also the CEO of eBaruwa.com, a platform dedicated to providing high-quality Statement of Purpose (SOP) writing services, website development at affordable rates, and a variety of engaging daily short stories to inspire and educate.

Currently pursuing a degree in Network and Communications System at Adventist University of Central Africa (AUCA), Sano has a strong passion for technology and continuous learning. He holds a certification in Network Essentials, which complements his academic pursuits and enhances his technical proficiency. Sano's career goals include becoming a systems administrator or network engineer, with plans to obtain advanced certifications such as Cisco CCNA, CEH, and CHFI.

In addition to his academic and entrepreneurial endeavors, Sano is a professional basketball player, demonstrating his dedication to both mental and physical excellence. His leadership skills, strategic vision, and commitment to innovation make him a dynamic and influential figure in the fields of IT and real estate.

Sano Kabayiza Jules is driven by a desire to leverage technology to solve real-world problems and create value for his community. His diverse interests and expertise position him as a future leader poised to make significant contributions to the technology and real estate sectors.
